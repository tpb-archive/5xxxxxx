Adventure has a new name: Alexandra Fortune! Join this whip smart young lady as she sets out to 

find her missing grandfather and unravel the mystery of the Lunar Archipelago, a cluster of 

islands that contains remnants of every great civilization throughout history and hides a 

treasure of unimaginable worth. As you guide Alexandra and her friend Jay through this thrilling 

hidden object quest, you'll solve ingenious puzzles, enjoy visually stunning 3D locations and 

crack a mystery that will not only change Alexandra's life forever but has the potential to 

affect the entire world!
