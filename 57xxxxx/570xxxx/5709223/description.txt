After finding himself at the constant abuse of his best friend Bobby, Marty has become fed up with his friend's twisted ways.
His girlfriend, a victim of Bobby's often cruel ways, couldn't agree more and they strategize murdering
Bobby, with a group of willing and unwilling participants in a small Florida town.
In the midst of their plotting, they find themselves contemplating with the possible aftermath of what could happen
