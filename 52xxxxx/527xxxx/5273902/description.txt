Heart of America (also called Home Room: Heart Of America or simply Home Room; not to be confused with the 2002 film Home_Room_(film)) is a 2003 drama film by German director Uwe Boll about a fictional school shooting in a suburban high school. It is believed to have been inspired by such shootings as the Columbine High School Massacre. The film is MPAA Rated R for violence, drug use, sexuality and language, all involving teens.
